# Shrimp salad with egg and corn
## Ingredient
- 1/2 lb cooked shrimp
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup corn
- diced garlic 蒜末
- millet pepper 小米辣
- chili powder 辣椒面
- white seasme 白芝麻
- light soy sauce 生抽
- vinegar 陈醋
- Oyster sauce 耗油
- salt

## Steps
1. Steam/boil eggs, corn, and shrimp and put in a large bowl 虾仁，鸡蛋，玉米煮熟放入碗中
2. Mix the above ingredients than add shrimp, eggs, and corns 淋上酱汁拌匀
